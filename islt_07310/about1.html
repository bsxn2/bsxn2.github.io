Educator Credentials

My name is Brianna Shipp and I have worked as an educator in a public school district for seven years. I am a mother of two daughters. I have a Master's degree in Educational Technology from Lindenwood University. I attended Harris Stowe State University, where I earned my Bachelor's in Education with an emphasis in Social Sciences. Currently, I am a member of the School of Information Science and Learning Technologies and working on my Ed. S degree in Learning Technology from the University of Missouri. I am certified to teach grades 5-9 and have worked with middle school students in grades six, seven, and eight. Throughout my career, I have developed a passion for presenting my students with the opportunity to enhance their learning using technology. Recently, I have developed the desire to aid fellow educators in expanding their pedagogy to include strategies and tools centered around technology incorporation in the classroom.
